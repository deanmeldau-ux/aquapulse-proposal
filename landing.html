Why the water works

All the good of a proper workout. None of the pounding.

Water carries up to ninety percent of your body weight. So you move freely, build real strength and get your heart going, while your knees, hips and back get a break. If running hurts, if you are starting over, or if you just want to feel good again, this is made for you.

Kind to your joints

Full body toning, balance and cardio with almost no impact. Perfect if you have sore knees, a stiff back, or you are easing back into movement.

High energy, real results

Great music, clear coaching and water resistance that works every muscle. You leave lighter than you arrived, and a little bit stronger every week.

A friendly crowd

Small groups where people know your name. You arrive as a stranger and leave with a few new friends. That is what keeps people coming back.

What a class is like

Your first 45 minutes, start to finish.

No guesswork, no gym intimidation. Here is exactly how it goes.

1

Arrive and settle in

Get there a few minutes early. Change, pop your things down, and step into the warm shallow water. Heather greets everyone and checks in with anyone new.

2

Gentle warm up

We loosen up with easy movements to the music. Nothing sudden, nothing scary. You feel your body wake up while the water holds you steady.

3

The main set

A full body mix of strength, cardio and balance, all at your own pace. Go harder or ease off whenever you like. Heather shows an easier and a stronger option for every move.

4

Cool down and chat

We wind down, stretch out in the water and catch our breath. You climb out feeling loose, clear headed and ready for the day. The kettle is usually on.

Good to know

Your questions, answered.

Do I need to be able to swim?

Not at all. Classes are in shallow water where you can stand comfortably the whole time. Your feet stay on the floor. If you can stand in a pool, you can do this.

What should I bring?

A swimming costume, a towel, and something warm for after. That is it. All the pool equipment is provided. A pair of goggles is optional and honestly not needed for these classes.

I am not fit. Is that a problem?

That is exactly who these classes are built for. You work at your own pace, and Heather shows an easier and a harder version of every move. Most people find the water does a lot of the work for them.

How much is it and how do I pay?

You pay for your first class to try it, then a monthly package brings the price down if you want to keep coming. Payment goes straight to Heather through her own secure card link or SnapScan. Prices are on the pricing section above.

How do I book?

Tap any book button on this page. It opens a WhatsApp message to Heather with your details started for you. She will reply, confirm a date with space, and hold your spot. Quick and human.

Can I come if I am pregnant?

Heather also runs a dedicated Prenatal Fitness class at 10am on the same days, designed for pregnancy. Message her and she will point you to the right class for where you are.

What if I need to miss a class?

Just let Heather know on WhatsApp. Classes are flexible with no lock in, so life happening is never a problem.
