# RESULT

## Summary
Built a polished, mobile-first, print-friendly two-page website/booklet for AquaPulse Aqua Fitness & Wellness (Heather Brown, Cape Town): a gorgeous **Partnership Proposal** aimed at venues, plus a companion **Growth Blueprint** with a scale strategy and realistic ZAR financial projections. The design matches her real brand exactly (palette sampled from her own logo, her logo/photos pulled from the live site), contains zero em/en dashes, and is published live on GitHub Pages (verified HTTP 200).

## Live links
- **Live site:** https://deanmeldau-ux.github.io/aquapulse-proposal/
- **Blueprint page:** https://deanmeldau-ux.github.io/aquapulse-proposal/blueprint.html
- **Repo:** https://github.com/deanmeldau-ux/aquapulse-proposal

## Files created
- `index.html` The Partnership Proposal (cover, vision/mission, the AquaPulse difference, class offerings, who it's for, benefits to the venue, partnership models, instructor bio, testimonials, gallery, community & events, CTA + contact).
- `blueprint.html` The Growth Blueprint (5-stage business model ladder, 9 revenue streams, differentiation/moats, unit economics + 3 scenarios with ZAR tables and CSS charts, 12-month roadmap, 3-year vision, path to a sellable asset).
- `styles.css` Full design system built on her sampled brand colours (navy #003261, ocean #057dcd, sky #43b0f1, aqua #99eeff, paper #f2eeeb).
- `app.js` Mobile nav, scroll-reveal animations, back-to-top.
- `assets/` logo-transparent.png, logo.jpg, heather.jpg, class-hero.jpg, favicon.png, plus her two brand icons (1.png, 2.png).
- `README.md`, `.nojekyll`.

## How to view / next steps
- Open the live link above on any phone or desktop.
- To make a PDF booklet: open a page, Print, "Save as PDF", enable background graphics.
- Heather personalises the clearly-marked `[Add your ...]` placeholders: certifications/insurance, three testimonials, gallery photos, email address and social handles.
