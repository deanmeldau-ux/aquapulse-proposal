Vision & Mission

Movement that meets people exactly where they are.

AquaPulse was born from a simple belief. Everybody deserves to move, to feel strong, and to enjoy it, no matter their age, their weight, or where they are on their health journey. Water makes that possible. It holds you, it challenges you, and it lets you rediscover what your body can do.

Our Vision

To become Cape Town's most loved aqua wellness brand, a name that families, doctors and venues trust to make movement joyful, safe and genuinely life changing.

Our Mission

To deliver classes that are fun, energetic and kind to the body, building a community where people arrive as strangers and leave as friends, and where every venue we partner with becomes a place people love to return to.

The AquaPulse Difference

Not just a class. A feeling people come back for.

Plenty of places have a pool. Very few turn that water into an experience. Here is what sets AquaPulse apart.

1

Joint friendly by design

Water carries up to 90% of your body weight, so every movement is gentle on knees, hips and backs while still delivering a real, full body workout. People who cannot run can thrive here.

2

Energy you can feel

Great music, clear coaching and a genuinely uplifting atmosphere. Members leave lighter than they arrived, which is exactly why they keep coming back.

3

Truly for everyone

Beginners, seniors, expectant mums, people in rehab and seasoned athletes all train side by side, each working at their own level in the same welcoming space.

4

Community at the core

We build regulars, not just attendance. Familiar faces, birthdays remembered, progress celebrated. That belonging is what keeps a programme full.

Class Offerings & Benefits

One pool. A programme for every body.

Each class blends strength, balance, cardio, mobility and confidence, all in the safe embrace of the water.

Signature

Aqua Fitness

Our flagship full body class. Energising, rhythmic and endlessly scalable, it builds strength and stamina while burning calories and toning, all without the strain.

  • Cardio and strength in one session
  • Boosts mood, energy and sleep
  • Improves balance and coordination
  • Suitable for all ages and fitness levels
Prenatal

Prenatal Aqua

A gentle, supportive class for expectant mums. Water takes the pressure off the spine and pelvis, easing aches while keeping mum active, strong and calm.

  • Relieves back and joint pressure
  • Safe, low impact conditioning
  • Supports circulation and swelling
  • A community of mums to lean on
Seniors

Active Ageing

Designed for older adults who want to stay mobile, independent and social. Warm water, steady pacing and a focus on the movements that matter for daily life.

  • Protects joints and bone health
  • Improves balance to reduce fall risk
  • Keeps muscles strong for daily living
  • Social connection that lifts the spirit
Rehab & Recovery

Gentle Restore

A slower, restorative class for those recovering from injury or surgery, or living with arthritis and chronic pain. Movement that heals rather than hurts.

  • Rebuilds strength after injury
  • Eases arthritis and chronic pain
  • Improves range of motion safely
  • Works beautifully alongside physio

Every class delivers the same six wins

Strength. Balance. Cardio. Mobility. Confidence. Community. The intensity flexes to the person, but the benefits are always there.

How The Partnership Works

Flexible terms. One shared goal.

We keep it simple and fair. Choose the model that fits your venue, and we can always adjust as the programme grows.

Model A

Venue Hire

A fixed fee per class or per hour

You rent the pool to AquaPulse for agreed time slots. We handle all bookings and payments, and keep the class income. Predictable and effortless for you.

Most popular

Model B

Revenue Share

An agreed split per head

We share class income on a percentage basis. Your reward grows as the classes fill, so we are both pulling in the same direction from day one.

Model C

Amenity Partnership

A retainer or bundled service

Ideal for estates, villages and hotels. AquaPulse becomes your in house wellness offering for residents or guests, funded as a facility amenity.
