# AquaPulse Aqua Fitness & Wellness

Partnership proposal and growth blueprint for **AquaPulse**, an aqua fitness and wellness brand run by Heather Brown in Cape Town, South Africa.

**Tagline:** Making waves in wellness. Low impact, high energy, 100% fun.

## What is in here

- **`index.html`** The Partnership Proposal. A gorgeous, mobile first booklet aimed at pools, estates, retirement villages, hotels and wellness venues. Cover, vision and mission, the AquaPulse difference, class offerings, who it is for, benefits to the venue, partnership models, instructor bio, testimonials, gallery, community plan and contact.
- **`blueprint.html`** The Growth Blueprint. A scale and wealth strategy: business model ladder, nine revenue streams, differentiation, honest ZAR financials with three scenarios, a 12 month roadmap and a 3 year vision.
- **`styles.css`** Shared design system, built on the exact brand colours sampled from Heather's own logo.
- **`app.js`** Light interactions (mobile nav, scroll reveal, back to top).
- **`assets/`** Logo, instructor photo, class photo and favicon pulled from the live site.

## Design notes

- Brand palette taken straight from the AquaPulse logo: navy `#003261`, ocean `#057dcd`, sky `#43b0f1`, aqua `#99eeff` on warm paper `#f2eeeb`.
- Fonts: Poppins for headings, Inter for body.
- Fully responsive, print friendly (use your browser Print to PDF to make a booklet).
- Placeholders are clearly marked `[Add your ...]` for Heather to personalise (credentials, testimonials, gallery photos, email, socials).

## Viewing

Open `index.html` in any browser, or visit the live GitHub Pages site linked in the repository description.

## To make a PDF booklet

Open either page, choose Print, set destination to Save as PDF, enable background graphics, and save.
